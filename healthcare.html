Regulatory Familiarity

Familiar with HCAI (OSHPD) inspections

California healthcare facilities fall under the oversight of HCAI — the Department of Health Care Access and Information (formerly OSHPD). We're familiar with the inspection expectations and documentation involved, so your project team, facilities director, or inspector of record has a roofing contractor that understands the environment.

  • HCAI / OSHPD familiarity — we understand inspection and documentation expectations for healthcare facilities.
  • Detailed inspection reports — photo-documented roof condition reports for facilities and compliance files.
  • Avetta-compliant safety — we follow Avetta protocols and keep compliance records current.
  • Qualified crews — trained and equipped for any roofing or waterproofing scope on a care campus.
  • Infection-control awareness — mindful of dust, debris, and access controls around patient areas.
