About Superior Roofing & Waterproofing

A locally owned, licensed San Diego roofing contractor built on quality workmanship and doing right by our customers.

Who We Are

San Diego roofing, done the right way

Superior Roofing & Waterproofing LLC is a licensed roofing and waterproofing contractor based in San Diego, California. We serve homeowners, businesses, and property managers across San Diego County with the full range of roofing services — from repairs and re-roofs to waterproofing and coatings.

Our name reflects our standard. We believe a roof is only as good as the crew that installs it and the company that stands behind it. That's why we focus on skilled workmanship, quality materials, and clear communication on every job — big or small.
