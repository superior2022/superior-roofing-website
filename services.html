Roofing & Waterproofing Services in San Diego

Full-service residential and commercial roofing for San Diego County — repairs, replacements, waterproofing, coatings, and maintenance under one licensed roof.

Residential

Residential Roofing

Whether you need a full re-roof or a section repaired, we install and service shingle, tile, and flat roofs on San Diego homes. We help you choose the right system for your home, budget, and the local climate — then install it cleanly and back it with warranties.

  • Asphalt shingle, concrete & clay tile, and flat roofs
  • Full tear-off and re-roof installations
  • Skylights, flashing, and ventilation
  • Manufacturer and workmanship warranties
Commercial

Commercial Roofing

We keep San Diego businesses and properties protected with durable low-slope and flat roofing systems. From TPO and single-ply membranes to built-up roofs, we deliver dependable installations and service that minimize disruption to your operations.

  • TPO, PVC, and single-ply membrane systems
  • Flat and low-slope roof replacement
  • Property management & multi-site service
  • Scheduled to work around your business hours
Repairs

Roof Repair & Leak Detection

A small leak today can mean major water damage tomorrow. Our team tracks down the true source of the problem — not just the symptom — and makes lasting repairs on residential and commercial roofs throughout San Diego County.

  • Leak detection and moisture troubleshooting
  • Storm, wind, and age-related damage repair
  • Flashing, valley, and penetration repairs
  • Emergency response for active leaks
Waterproofing

Waterproofing

Waterproofing is in our name for a reason. We protect decks, balconies, planters, and flat surfaces with proven coatings and membrane systems that keep water where it belongs — outside your building.

  • Deck & balcony waterproof coatings
  • Below-grade and planter waterproofing
  • Elastomeric and membrane systems
  • Sealants, flashing, and detail work
Coatings

Roof Coatings & Restoration

Not every aging roof needs to be torn off. Reflective roof coatings can restore a sound flat roof, stop leaks, and lower cooling costs in San Diego's sunny climate — often at a fraction of replacement cost.

  • Silicone and acrylic reflective coatings
  • Energy-saving "cool roof" systems
  • Restoration of existing flat roofs
  • Extended roof life and warranty options
Maintenance

Inspections & Preventive Maintenance

Regular inspections and maintenance are the cheapest way to protect your roof. We catch small issues before they become expensive repairs and provide clear reports you can act on — ideal for homeowners and property managers alike.

  • Detailed roof condition inspections
  • Preventive maintenance plans
  • Pre-purchase and warranty inspections
  • Documentation and photo reporting
